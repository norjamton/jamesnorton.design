About

I have over five years of experience designing and building digital products, working across in-house product teams, consultancies and as a freelancer. I’ve partnered with scale-ups, FTSE 250 companies and independents across sectors like fintech, marketplaces, ticketing, the arts, charities and government.

‍

I started my career as a UX Designer at Ten4, before transitioning into end-to-end product design and later specialising in UI and Design Systems at Soldo, shortly after their $180M Series C.

‍

Now I'm one of three Product Designers at RA, where I own UI and design systems while collaborating across project lifecycles. I work on both the consumer platform, ra.co, and the B2B offering, RA Pro, across our web, iOS, and Android properties.

‍

I also build Webflow sites for freelance and side projects, and occasionally at work as part of product marketing and growth efforts.
