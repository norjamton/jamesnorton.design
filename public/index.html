About

I'm James — a Senior Product Designer with 6+ years' experience helping businesses scale with strategic design. I've worked across marketplaces, fintech and agencies, leading projects end-to-end and collaborating closely with product, engineering and growth teams.

‍

Currently I'm at RA, the world's largest electronic music platform, designing for millions of users across both the consumer marketplace and RA Pro. I balance the needs of fans looking for their next event with promoters managing complex ticketing and financial operations, while leading UI and design systems across web and mobile.

‍

Previously I worked at Soldo following their Series C, designing complex B2B fintech products, and at Ten4, a digital agency specialising in ticketing, donations and memberships.

‍

Over time I've learned that to move fast and maintain quality at scale, you need strong systems. That's where I do my best work — bringing together strategy and data with visual craft, design systems and technical understanding to help teams ship the right thing quickly.

‍

Outside work, you'll find me building Wordwise, an AI-powered Kindle companion, and cycling around London.
